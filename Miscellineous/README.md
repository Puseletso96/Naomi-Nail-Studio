## Business Description
Naomi Nail Studio is a beauty business located in Unit 14, Mmabatho, Mafikeng, North West. The studio specializes in professional nail care services including manicures, pedicures, acrylic nails, gel nails, and nail art designs.

The goal of the business is to provide high-quality, affordable, and creative nail services in a clean and welcoming environment.



## Website Pages

### 1. Home Page
The homepage introduces Naomi Nail Studio and welcomes visitors. It gives a brief overview of services and sets the tone of the business.



### 2. About Us Page
The About Us page explains:
- Who Naomi Nail Studio is  
- The mission of the business  
- The vision for growth  
- Core values such as professionalism, cleanliness, creativity, and customer satisfaction  

---

### 3. Gallery Page
The gallery displays images of nail work including:
- Acrylic nails  
- Gel nails  
- Manicure designs  
- Nail art  

This page helps clients see examples of the services offered.
