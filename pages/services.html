Our Services

At Naomi Nail-Studio, we offer a wide range of nail care services to meet your needs. Our skilled technicians are dedicated to providing you with the best experience possible. Here are some of the services we offer:

  • Manicures: Our manicures include nail shaping, cuticle care, hand massage, and polish application. We offer a variety of polish colors and finishes to choose from.
  • Pedicures: Our pedicures include foot soaking, exfoliation, nail trimming, cuticle care, foot massage, and polish application. We use high-quality products to ensure your feet are soft and beautiful.
  • Nail Art: Express your creativity with our nail art services. We can create custom designs, patterns, and embellishments to make your nails stand out.
  • Gel Nails: Our gel nail services provide long-lasting color and shine. Gel nails are perfect for those who want a durable and chip-resistant manicure.
  • poly-gel based Nails: For those looking for added length and strength, our poly-gel nail services are a great option. We can customize the shape and design to suit your preferences.
